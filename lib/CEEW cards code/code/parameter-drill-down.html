Solarization of Feeder
Suitable

Given the high power purchase rate for the respective DISCOM, extent feeder segregation, and penetration of electric pumps, solarization of feeder would be the way to promote solar-powered irrigation in Anantapur. It would ensure rapid and efficient solarization of irrigation power at large scale.
